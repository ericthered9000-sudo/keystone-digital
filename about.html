The Story

I'm James. I'm 51 years old, married to my wife Becky for 30 years, and proud dad to Aislinn (she's a junior at University of Alabama, pre-law, 4.16 GPA — yeah, I brag).

For 20+ years, I worked physical jobs. Walmart produce departments — loading 40-60 lb crates, walking 10-21k steps a day. Before that, I was a correctional officer at Kentucky State Penitentiary. Before that, industrial supply, building tools for machining processes.

I've been into computers since the DOS days. Learned some C++ when it came out. But life happened — bills, family, the grind. Tech was something other people did.

Then I saw AI and agents taking off. I realized: physical work is taking a toll on my body, and this might be my one shot to get ahead of it. Not just learn to code — build things that matter.

Why Keystone Digital?

Every app we build starts from a real problem

🏠

HomeBeacon

My mom was getting older. I wanted to know she was okay without calling her 5 times a day. She wanted independence. We both wanted peace of mind.

HomeBeacon was built for her. Now it's for every family caring for aging parents.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

FamilyShift

When you're coordinating care for a parent, siblings are spread out. Who did what? Who's handling the next doctor visit? The group chat chaos is real.

FamilyShift is for families who need to stay on the same page without the headache.

🤖

AgentHub

Local businesses — HVAC, mechanics, nail salons, coffee shops — are drowning in admin work. Answering calls, booking appointments, sending follow-ups.

AgentHub gives them AI receptionists that actually work, at prices small businesses can afford.

🎴

Tarot Decision App

My daughter Aislinn loves tarot. I saw the subscription traps everywhere — $9.99/month for basic readings. That's predatory.

Our tarot app is one-time purchase. No subscriptions. Fair pricing. Built different.
