About Keystone Digital

Keystone Digital was founded by James, a dad from Princeton, Kentucky who spent 20+ years working physical jobs — Walmart produce departments, correctional officer, industrial supply.

After two decades of lifting 40-60 lb crates and walking 10-21k steps a day, James saw AI and agents as the future of work. He wanted to get ahead of it — not just learn to code, but build things that matter.

Every app we build starts from a real problem: HomeBeacon for his mom's wellness, FamilyShift for coordinating family care, AgentHub for local businesses drowning in admin work.

This isn't Silicon Valley tech. This is blue-collar tech — built by workers, for workers. No corporate jargon, no startup-bro energy, just useful tools for real people.
