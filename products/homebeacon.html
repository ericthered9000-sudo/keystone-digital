The HomeBeacon Approach

Wellness monitoring without the creep factor

📊

Daily Wellness Scores

HomeBeacon tracks daily activities — medication adherence, meal logs, mood check-ins, sleep patterns — and generates a simple wellness score.

Green = all good. Yellow = check in. Red = reach out now. No surveillance, just insights.

🔔

Pattern Alerts

HomeBeacon learns your parent's routines and alerts you to concerning patterns — missed meds for 3 days, no meal logs, unusual sleep changes.

You're notified before small issues become emergencies.

💊

Medication Reminders

Set up medication schedules with custom reminders. HomeBeacon tracks adherence and shows you the history.

Know if mom's taking her meds without having to ask every day.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Sharing

Invite family members with a code. Everyone sees the same wellness data, so you can coordinate care without group chat chaos.

Built for families, not just individuals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is this medical monitoring?

No. HomeBeacon is a wellness app, not a medical device. We don't diagnose conditions or replace doctor visits. We help families stay connected and notice patterns — but we're not a substitute for professional medical care.

Does my parent need a smartphone?

Yes, the senior uses the HomeBeacon app to log activities. If they don't have a smartphone, you can log on their behalf during visits or calls. We're working on simplified modes for less tech-savvy users.

What about privacy?

Privacy is core to HomeBeacon. The senior controls what's shared. Data is encrypted. We don't sell your data. Ever. See our full privacy policy for details.

Can I use this for multiple parents?

Yes! The Family plan supports 2 seniors, and Premium supports unlimited. Perfect for families caring for both parents, or professional caregivers managing multiple clients.

Is there an iOS app?

Android is available now. iOS is in development — we're building it right. Join our email list to be notified when it launches.

What if we need help?

Email us at support@keystonedigitalcorp.com. We respond within 24 hours. Family and Premium plans get priority support.
