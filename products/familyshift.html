The Caregiver Coordination Problem

When multiple family members are involved, things get messy fast

📱

Group Chat Chaos

"Who took mom to her appointment?" "Did someone refill her meds?" "What did the doctor say?" Important info gets lost in the noise.

📅

Scheduling Nightmares

Doctor appointments, pharmacy runs, grocery shopping, social visits. Coordinating across siblings' schedules is a full-time job.

💰

Expense Confusion

Who paid for what? Who owes who? Medical bills, groceries, gas money. The financial tracking becomes its own headache.

The FamilyShift Solution

One place for everything. Everyone on the same page.

📅

Shared Care Calendar

See all appointments, visits, and tasks in one calendar. Color-coded by family member. No more double-booking or missed appointments.

Sync with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or use standalone.

✅

Task Assignments

Assign tasks to family members: "Pick up meds," "Grocery shopping," "Saturday visit." Track completion, send reminders.

Optional gentle nudges — no guilt trips.

📓

Care Journal

Log daily notes: "Mom seemed tired today," "Ate well," "Good mood after church." Family members can read and add their own observations.

Pattern insights over time — see how mom's doing at a glance.

💰

Expense Tracking

Log caregiving expenses: gas, meds, medical bills, groceries. Split costs fairly. Export for tax deductions.

Transparent, no awkward money conversations.

📋

Document Vault

Store important documents: insurance cards, medication lists, advance directives, doctor contacts. Secure, encrypted, accessible to authorized family.

Everything in one place during emergencies.

💬

Family Updates

Post updates that notify all family members: "Doctor visit summary," "Medication change," "Good news!" No more forwarding texts.

Optional integration with HomeBeacon wellness data.
